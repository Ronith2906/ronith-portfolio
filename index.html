About

A builder-analyst hybrid

I'm a Senior Business Systems Analyst with 8+ years across healthcare, financial services, consulting, and retail — currently at McKesson, previously at Deloitte and Smartried Technologies.

What I do best: sit between business stakeholders and engineering teams and make sure what gets built is what was actually needed. That means running JAD sessions, writing user stories that don't get rewritten three sprints later, doing the gap analysis nobody wants to do, and turning vague exec asks into shipped pipelines and dashboards that move numbers.

Hands-on across the modern data stack — AWS (Redshift, S3, Glue, Athena), GCP (BigQuery, Dataflow), Snowflake, Databricks, and Spark/Scala for the heavier transformation work. The BA skill set sharpens the technical work; the technical work sharpens the BA work.

On the side I'm building SDR Jarvis — an AI outbound email platform for solo founders doing B2B sales without a dedicated SDR. Best way I've found to stay close to the implementation side of the work I spec out for a living.
